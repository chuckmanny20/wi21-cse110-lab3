My Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

  1. Take out pot, milk, butter, and Kraft mac n cheese box.
  2. Pour 8 cups of water into pot and boil.
  3. Add macaroni to pot and cook for 7-8 minutes.
  4. Take pot off heat and drain water from pot.
  5. Add cheese power, 1/4 cup of milk, and 2 tbsp of butter to macaroni.
  6. Stir thoroughly and Enjoy!
